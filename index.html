Sensor Fusion with Single-Photon Detectors | Advisor: Prof. Matthew O’Toole

  • Exploring and developing a suite of sensor fusion techniques around an emerging sensing technology known as a single-photon avalanche diode, or SPAD.
  • Working on a deep sensor fusion approach to combine multiple modalities, particularly a Single Photon Lidar, a Stereo camera and a Radar to obtain high-resolution depth maps (3D representation of the environment).
